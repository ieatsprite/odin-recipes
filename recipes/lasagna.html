Lasagna

Description

Great recipe I found online that tastes delicious, and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Cheese of your choice
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 2-3 sheets of lasagna

Steps

  1. Roll lasagna up stuffing in the beef.
  2. Bake for 30 mins in the oven at 425 degrees F.
  3. Remove from oven, top with cheese and bake for another 15 minutes.
