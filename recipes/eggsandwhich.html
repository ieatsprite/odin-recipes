Egg Sandwhich

Description

My favorite sandwhich to make for lunch.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Hardboiled Eggs
  • Diced chicken
  • Mustard
  • Onions
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. This recipe uses precooked canned chicken, so prepare chicken by placing into a mixing bowl.
  2. Take the eggs, mash together and place into the mixing bowl.
  3. Combine the rest of the ingredients, mayo, mustard and onions into the mixing bowl.
  4. Place on your bread of choice and enjoy!
