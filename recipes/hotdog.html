Hot Dog

Description

A classic hot dog recipe that is on par with Dodger Dogs!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Hot Dogs
  • Relish
  • Mustard
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Grill hot dogs for about 8 minutes on high heat.
  2. Toast the buns for 30 seconds in a mircowave or on the grill.
  3. Spread mustard and relish evenly across buns, placing hot dog in between buns, then topping with onion.
